Stops Those Ads!

Nov 28th, 2015 8:10 pm

Javascript is definitely a programming language that improved the user experience while navigate through websites, as it avoids slow webpage reloads for any action. This advantage those comes also with some trouble as Javascript is also the cause of “below the surface” behavior. One of which is ceratinly the presence of ads, in any website we visit. The solution to this annoying problem that spoils our experience online has been brought by many application which intentionally block ads from appearing on the screen. But there is a faster solution, that requires no installation of any third party application. Javascript! Well, the avoidance of having Javascript running! And it’s as easy as one (or two) clicks on the browser’s settings.

If you have Chrome. here is a two-easy step procedure:

Here we go! No Javascript! I first wonder what would happen, thinking that most of the websites would not even load … instead it’s pretty much the opposite. Most of the websites work perfectly fine! and they are not so intrusive! Ads are not there to distract you! Yes, they leave you alone while you are reading the news for example!

There are also other advantages that comes with this. Turning off Javascipt limits the possibilities of having your data be collected by third parties. There are no more “listeners” around the page!

I started reading online if this was just a geeky-sort-of experience, but instead I found out that there is even an entire movement that launched a “free Javascript” campaign in 2013. The group wants websites to use open source Javascript source so people can “understand” and control the code that runs on their computer. Failing to provide that, they would like to force companies to create websites that are fully functionaal even without Javascript.

As I mentioned before most of the websites already work just fine. Some, such as Google Docs are obviously not performing. Overall it’s a pretty quiet navigation out there on the web, without Javascript. No flashing, annoying Ads popping up in the middle of an article! another small advantage that I read about, but I didn’t have the chance to test is the fact that the lapto battery is supposed to last longer…!
